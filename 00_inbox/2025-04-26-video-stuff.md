# video stuff

Got it — you want **retro/vintage/lofi** visuals (no people) to match your **lofi R&B beats** on TikTok, without wasting time editing or creating visuals yourself. You just want to **focus on production**.
I'll suggest some sources where you can get a **huge collection** of public domain or free-to-use vintage clips that match your vibe.

Here’s the list, with **reasons** why they fit your description:

---

### 1. **Pond5 Public Domain Project**
- **Website:** [Pond5 Public Domain](https://www.pond5.com/free)
- **Why:**
- Huge collection of **vintage footage**, including **old films, landscapes, cityscapes, nature, vintage technology**, etc.
- **No licensing issues** — totally public domain.
- You can easily find **no-humans** clips (like city skylines, ocean waves, retro TVs, neon signs).

---

### 2. **Prelinger Archives (via Internet Archive)**
- **Website:** [Prelinger Archive on Archive.org](https://archive.org/details/prelinger)
- **Why:**
- Massive library of **vintage educational films, commercials, old landscapes**.
- A lot of **retro textures** like "old cars," "1950s city life," **abstract lights** — perfect for lo-fi R&B.
- Mostly **public domain** or free to use with attribution.

---

### 3. **Public Domain Review (Moving Images Section)**
- **Website:** [Public Domain Review Films](https://publicdomainreview.org/collections?type=film)
- **Why:**
- Curated high-quality **vintage, strange, aesthetic** films.
- Very **artsy and dreamy**, perfect for atmospheric lo-fi beats.
- Easy to find visuals **without people** — abstract patterns, nature shots, machines.

---

### 4. **Videvo (Public Domain and CC0 Section)**
- **Website:** [Videvo Free Stock Footage](https://www.videvo.net/stock-video-footage/free/)
- **Why:**
- They tag footage clearly as **"Public Domain"** or **"CC0"** (no credit needed).
- You’ll find **vintage film grains, VHS glitches, city time-lapses, foggy forests**, old projectors.
- Good quality for TikTok (you'll need 9:16 crop but still easy).

---

### 5. **Mazwai (Curated Free Video Clips)**
- **Website:** [Mazwai Free Videos](https://mazwai.com/)
- **Why:**
- Fewer clips but very **artistic and ambient**.
- Loops well for **40s beat videos**.
- Many videos show **nature, city lights, old tech** — usually no humans in frame.

---

## **Bonus Tip:**
You can add a **subtle VHS effect or grain overlay** (tons of free ones out there) to **glue** your beat and video together even tighter without needing complicated editing. Even free apps like **CapCut** or **VN Editor** can do that super easily.

---

**Summary why these fit you:**
- **Public Domain or CC0** = no copyright worries.
- **Retro/vintage visuals** = matches lofi RnB beat vibe.
- **No people** = focus on landscapes, cityscapes, abstract motion.
- **Large collections** = enough for you to post a LOT (40s loops) without recycling visuals too much.
- **Low editing effort** = you can stay focused on **making beats**.
