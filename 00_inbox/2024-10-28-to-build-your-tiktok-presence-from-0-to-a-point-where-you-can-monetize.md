# To build your TikTok presence from 0 to a point where you can monetize…

To build your TikTok presence from 0 to a point where you can monetize, here’s a focused plan:

### Weekly Content Plan

**1. Posting Frequency:**
- Aim for **3-5 videos per week**. Consistency is key, and this frequency allows you to test different types of content.

**2. Types of Videos:**
- **Beat Previews:** Snippets of your beats (15-30 seconds).
- **Tutorials:** Quick tips on beat-making (30-60 seconds).
- **Challenges/Trends:** Participate in trending sounds/challenges related to music.
- **Behind-the-Scenes:** Show your production setup or workflow.

### Engagement Metrics

To grow your audience and eventually monetize, focus on achieving the following engagement metrics:

**1. Views:**
- Aim for at least **1,000 views per video** within the first 24-48 hours. This can vary based on the topic and timing.

**2. Likes:**
- Target **10-20%** of your views as likes. For example, if your video gets 1,000 views, aim for **100-200 likes**.

**3. Comments:**
- Engage your audience to get at least **5-10 comments** per video. Ask questions or encourage feedback to boost interaction.

**4. Shares:**
- Aim for **5-10 shares** per video, which helps extend your reach.

### Path to Monetization

**1. Build a Following:**
- Aim to reach **1,000 followers** as a baseline. With this follower count, you can start monetizing your account.

**2. Engagement Rate:**
- A good engagement rate on TikTok is **3-5%**. Focus on creating content that resonates with your audience to maintain or exceed this.

**3. Timeframe for Monetization:**
- Typically, you can start seeing monetization options (like the TikTok Creator Fund, brand deals, or affiliate marketing) after about **3-6 months** of consistent posting and engagement, assuming you hit the follower and engagement metrics.

**4. Diversify Income Streams:**
- **Affiliate Marketing:** Promote music production tools or courses.
- **Selling Beats:** Use your TikTok to drive traffic to a platform where you sell your beats (e.g., your own website or BeatStars).
- **Brand Collaborations:** Once you establish a following, reach out to brands for potential partnerships.

### Summary Steps

1. **Create and post 3-5 videos per week** focusing on engaging content.
2. **Achieve 1,000 views**, 100-200 likes, and 5-10 comments per video within the first few days.
3. **Grow your follower count to 1,000** within 3-6 months.
4. **Explore monetization options** such as the Creator Fund and brand collaborations once you establish a solid engagement and follower base.

By maintaining consistency and focusing on engagement, you’ll be well on your way to monetizing your TikTok presence as a music producer.
