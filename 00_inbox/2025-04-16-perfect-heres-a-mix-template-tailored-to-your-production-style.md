# Perfect — here’s a **Mix Template** tailored to your production style…

Perfect — here’s a **Mix Template** tailored to your production style: aiming for **J Dilla / Monte Booker groove**, with **dub/garage influence**, and **Ivy Lab-level clarity and width**. This focuses on **EQ ranges, panning, levels, and FX chains** per group.

---

## **Hybrid Beat Mix Template**

### **1. Kick**
- **EQ**:
- Boost: 40–60Hz (sub)
- Dip: 300–400Hz (mud)
- Light boost: 2–3kHz (click/attack)
- **FX**:
- Optional light saturation (IVGI / Saturation Knob)
- **Panning**: Center
- **Level**: Around -6 to -8 dB peak
- **Sidechain**: Bass & pads

---

### **2. Snare / Clap**
- **EQ**:
- Boost: 180–220Hz (body), 1.5–3kHz (crack), 8–10kHz (air)
- **Transient shaper**: Boost attack, trim sustain
- **Reverb**:
- Short plate or dub-style delay (TAL Dub Delay or Supermassive)
- Send FX, not insert
- **Panning**: Center or 5–10% L/R if layered
- **Level**: Slightly under kick (-8 to -10 dB)

---

### **3. Hi-Hats / Percs**
- **EQ**:
- Low-cut below 200Hz
- Presence peak: 5–8kHz
- **Panning**:
- Hats: 20–40% L/R
- Percs: 30–75% L/R (auto-pan some)
- **FX**:
- Subtle delay or room verb
- **Level**: Sit just above the pad textures but under snare (-12 to -16 dB)

---

### **4. Bass / Sub**
- **EQ**:
- Boost: 40–90Hz
- Cut: 150–300Hz (mud)
- **Sidechain**: Duck against kick
- **FX**:
- Use distortion/saturation for upper harmonics (IVGI or CamelCrusher)
- **Panning**: Mono (centered)
- **Level**: Match kick power — watch LUFS

---

### **5. Chords / Pads / Samples**
- **EQ**:
- High-pass: 150–200Hz
- Notch cut: Where vocals or leads sit (500Hz–2kHz)
- **Stereo Imaging**: Wide (50–100%)
- **Reverb / Delay**:
- Long tails (Supermassive)
- Filtered delays (Frohmage or Kilohearts Filter)
- **Automation**:
- Reverb/delay mix
- Filter cutoff for dubby movement
- **Level**: Low-mid (-16 to -20 dB), floaty and supportive

---

### **6. Leads / Melodic Hits / Vocal Chops**
- **EQ**:
- Boost: 1–5kHz (presence)
- Cut: Any clashing mids
- **FX**:
- Ping-pong delay / spring reverb
- Saturate for presence
- **Panning**: Wide but center-weighted (dual track L/R if needed)
- **Level**: -10 to -14 dB depending on feature role

---

### **7. FX / Dub Drops / Risers**
- **EQ**:
- Sculpt based on content (carve lows or highs depending on focus)
- **FX**:
- Heavy delay, echo, tape warble (Vinyl, Supermassive)
- **Panning**: Creative! LCR movement encouraged
- **Level**: Just audible enough to fill space (-20 dB and below)

---

### **8. Bus/Master Chain (Optional)**
- **Drum Bus**:
- Light compression (SN01-G)
- Subtle saturation
- **Mix Bus / Master**:
- EQ (gentle tilt if needed)
- Glue compressor
- Limiter (Limiter No6) to hit -8 to -10 LUFS for streaming
- Youlean Loudness Meter to check loudness
