### Why These Fit Better
The original TikTok aesthetic is performative minimalism—every choice is for the camera.
This image reads as authentic minimalism—choices made for life, camera is incidental.
The gap isn’t in what’s worn, but why it’s worn. This aesthetic says “I don’t need to prove anything through clothing because my environment/achievements do it for me.” That’s a fundamentally different philosophy than “my curated basics signal my aspirational lifestyle brand.“????????????????
