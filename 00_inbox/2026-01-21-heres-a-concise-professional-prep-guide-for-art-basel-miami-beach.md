Art Basel Miami Beach is not just a single show — it’s the centerpiece of a week-long city-wide art ecosystem:

226+ galleries from around the world showing modern & contemporary art. (Art Basel)
Strong focus on historical + contemporary painting, sculpture, and major blue-chip artists. (Art Basel)

? Your Most Concise Roadmap to Art Basel 2026
### This is step-by-step, practical, and goal-oriented

1?? Foundation: Brand + Identity
a) Logo & Visual Identity
Design a clean logotype + wordmark that scales for web, print, cards, and signage.
Use neutral, gallery-friendly colors (black/white/gray) with one accent color.
Deliverables
Logo + favicon
Palette + typography
Brand guidelines PDF
b) Core Story / Artist Statement
2–3 sentences explaining who you are, what you make, and why it matters.
Tailor it to your niche (e.g., digital art, painting, mixed media).

2?? Portfolio Ready for Review
c) Curated Work Selection
For your first Basel, aim for quality over quantity:
10–20 digital pieces
Or 8–12 physical works (if applying to galleries)
Include titles, year, medium, dimensions
d) Professional Documentation
High-res images + flattened JPG for web
One-minute video clip or screen recording of digital work
Include a concise description for each piece

3?? Online Presence That Converts
e) Website / Online Store
Use platforms like Squarespace, Shopify, or Webflow:
Home page: clean branding + hero work
Portfolio/Gallery page
About / press / CV
Store: 10–30 products (prints, limited editions, merch)
Product Types: Fine art prints, limited digital editions, NFTs (optional), apparel/textiles with your art
Tip: Make purchasing easy — support credit cards + PayPal + digital wallets if doing NFTs.

4?? Social Media + Growth
f) Profiles
Instagram
X (Twitter)
TikTok or YouTube Shorts
g) Content Plan (Weekly)
3 posts per week (Work in progress, finished pieces, studio life)
1 Reel/Short per week (60–90 sec)
Engage artists/galleries with thoughtful comments
KPIs
2–5% engagement rate
Focus on niche art communities (#ContemporaryArt, #DigitalArt)

5?? Gallery & Fair Pitch Strategy
h) Target Galleries
Start with 20–40 galleries (mix established + emerging)
Look for ones that match your aesthetic
i) Pitch Package
### Email with
One-paragraph intro
3–5 key pieces (JPEG + link to online portfolio)
CV / exhibitions / press
Ask for a review meeting or inclusion in submissions
Follow-up
2 reminders over 2 weeks after first send

6?? Preparation for Basel Week
j) Travel/Booking
Miami bookings for early December (VIP Days are before public opening)
RSVP to gallery openings & industry events
k) Printed Materials
Business cards
Mini printed portfolio book
QR QR codes linking to your site/portfolio
l) Physical + Digital Presentation
### If showing digital work
Bring iPad / tablet + custom stand
USB + cloud backup

? Clinic Tips: How Art Basel Actually Works
? Sales vs. Networking

? Know the Culture
People attend for art, brand activations, fashion, and lifestyle — so be ready for multi-layer interactions. (Miami Condo Investments)
