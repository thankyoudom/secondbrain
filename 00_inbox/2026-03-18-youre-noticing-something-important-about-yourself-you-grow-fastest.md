# You’re noticing something important about yourself: you grow fastest…

You’re noticing something important about yourself: you grow fastest when the work is structured, repeatable, and compounds into something tangible. Not just “learning,” but stacking reps that turn into leverage.

LeetCode can be part of that—but if you make it your main thing, it’ll cap your upside given your goals (platform engineer ? data/infra ? long-term leadership). It sharpens thinking, but doesn’t directly build the systems intuition you actually need.

Let’s tighten this into a daily system that compounds.
