# Congrats on earning your GCP Cloud Associate Engineer certification…

Congrats on earning your GCP Cloud Associate Engineer certification! That's a great accomplishment, and you're already well on your way to mastering cloud technologies.
As for getting the Terraform Associate Certification after the GCP cert, most people typically spend 2 to 3 monthspreparing for the Terraform Associate exam, depending on their familiarity with infrastructure-as-code (IaC) tools and experience with Terraform. Since you're already familiar with cloud infrastructure, that might shorten your prep time.
How long does it take for others?
2 months: For those who already have hands-on experience with Terraform, it might only take a couple of months to review and solidify their knowledge.
3 months: For beginners or people transitioning from other IaC tools, it might take a bit longer to get comfortable with the syntax, concepts, and best practices.
Best Free Resources to Study for the Terraform Associate Certification:
Terraform Documentation (Official)
The Terraform documentation is one of the best places to learn, as it’s constantly updated with the latest information. You can go through the guides, tutorials, and reference materials to get a solid understanding of Terraform concepts.
Learn Terraform on HashiCorp’s Learn Platform
HashiCorp offers free, hands-on tutorials that are perfect for beginners and those preparing for the exam. They cover everything from the basics to more advanced topics, including managing infrastructure with Terraform.
YouTube
There are many free courses available on YouTube:
FreeCodeCamp and TechWorld with Nana offer great tutorials on Terraform basics.
Andrew Brown (ExamPro) has some excellent walkthroughs that include exam-focused material as well.
HashiCorp Certification Exam Study Guide
Official Terraform Associate Study Guide is available on HashiCorp's website. This guide outlines everything you need to know, including detailed exam objectives and links to relevant resources.
GitHub Repositories and Community Resources
Search for free repositories and study guides created by others on GitHub. For example, Terraform-Awesome is a curated list of Terraform resources.
Terraform CLI & Providers
Install Terraform and experiment by building real infrastructure in a test environment (AWS, Azure, or GCP). Hands-on practice is essential. You can create projects like:
Deploying a simple web server.
Setting up VPCs, subnets, and load balancers using Terraform.
Practice Exams
A Cloud Guru has a free trial that you can use to access Terraform courses and practice exams.
Another good option is ExamsRadar, which offers free Terraform practice questions.
Reddit Communities
Communities like r/Terraform and r/Azure (for cloud-specific Terraform usage) can provide insights and advice on preparation strategies.
Check out the Terraform subreddit for links to exam prep resources, discussions, and study guides.
Practice Labs (Free Tiers)
Many cloud providers like AWS, Azure, and GCP offer free tiers, and you can use them to practice your Terraform skills without incurring costs.
CloudAcademy (Free Trial)
CloudAcademy offers a Terraform course as well, and you can try it for free for a limited period. It has hands-on labs and quizzes to test your knowledge.

Do you already have any Terraform experience, or are you just starting with it? That could help narrow down which resources would be best for you!
