# Bet — here's a **12-week launch strategy** tailored for a music producer…

Bet — here's a **12-week launch strategy** tailored for a music producer building their brand from the ground up (or scaling up if you’ve already got a bit going). The focus: **consistent content, strategic outreach, audience building, and monetization** — all paced out so you don’t burn out.

---

# **12-Week Music Producer Launch Strategy**

## **PHASE 1: FOUNDATION & CONTENT SYSTEMS (Weeks 1–3)**

### **Week 1 – Setup & Brand Identity**
**Goal:** Lock in your brand identity & prepare content templates.

- Lock down your **@handles** + branding (IG, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter, BeatStars).
- Set up **Link-in-bio** (Koji, Beacons, etc.)
- Build 2–3 **Canva templates** for Instagram + YouTube thumbnails.
- Prepare **bio** for each platform (short, clear, value-driven).
- Define your **beat style lanes** (e.g., trap, drill, R&B, lo-fi).
- Create a **folder of 5 finished beats** to start posting.

**Deliverables by end of week:**
- Fully branded profiles
- 5 finished beats (with previews cut)
- 1–2 beat videos ready to post

---

### **Week 2 – Content Pipeline & First Posts**
**Goal:** Start building visibility with your first public content drops.

- Post your **first beat video** on TikTok, IG Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
- Launch your **first Type Beat** on YouTube (with SEO-optimized title).
- Add **download links or buy links** (BeatStars, etc.).
- Share a “making of the beat” or **breakdown clip**.

**Focus:**
- 3–4 short-form videos this week
- 1 long-form YouTube or full Type Beat
- Daily IG stories showing BTS/studio life

---

### **Week 3 – Engage & Outreach**
**Goal:** Start building real relationships and potential collaborations.

- DM 10 upcoming artists with a **custom message + beat offer.**
- Ex: “Yo I made this beat that I think fits your vibe. Lmk if you want to vibe on it.”
- Leave 10–15 **genuine comments daily** on artist/producer pages.
- Join 2–3 **Discord communities** or Reddit threads (active spots).

---

## **PHASE 2: CONSISTENCY & GROWTH (Weeks 4–8)**

### **Week 4–5 – Drop a Beat Pack / Freebie**
**Goal:** Build your email list + gain trust.

- Create a **free beat pack** (3 tagged beats).
- Use it as your **lead magnet** via your link-in-bio.
- Promote it in Reels, TikTok, and Stories.
- Announce via post: “Free beat pack for indie artists — limited to 100 downloads.”
- Add a simple opt-in form to collect emails (Mailchimp, Beehiiv, or Gumroad free download with email).

**Bonus:** Run a quick **beat contest** using those beats — best verse wins exclusive rights or shoutout.

---

### **Week 6–7 – Collab & Content Expansion**
**Goal:** Increase reach through collaboration & higher-value content.

- Reach out to 5 producers or artists for **collab beats or duets**.
- Drop a **tutorial video**: “How I Made This Trap Beat in FL Studio.”
- Release 1–2 **lo-fi or chill beat tapes** (Spotify/DistroKid).
- Continue posting 3–5x/week.

---

### **Week 8 – Launch Paid Beat Drops**
**Goal:** Monetize traffic & test pricing/offers.

- Drop your **first exclusive beat drop**:
- “5 exclusive beats dropping Friday — once they’re gone, they’re gone.”
- Offer bundle pricing: Buy 2 get 1 free.
- Post beat previews across TikTok/IG leading up to it.
- Send to your **email list** and message warm leads (from DMs).

---

## **PHASE 3: MOMENTUM & SCALING (Weeks 9–12)**

### **Week 9–10 – Testimonials, Case Studies & Social Proof**
**Goal:** Build trust with your growing audience.

- Ask 3–5 artists to **send you snippets or testimonials**.
- Post before/after style Reels: “The beat I sent vs the final track.”
- Feature artists using your beats in IG stories, feed, and TikTok.
- Create a **Highlight**: “Artists Using My Beats.”

---

### **Week 11 – Mini Campaign: Producer Identity**
**Goal:** Share your story and build brand personality.

- Post a mini-doc style clip: “Why I Produce” or “My First Studio Setup.”
- Do a “Top 3 Influences” series (3 short Reels/TikToks).
- Twitter threads or IG carousels: “3 Lessons I’ve Learned as a Producer.”

---

### **Week 12 – Big Drop / Live Launch**
**Goal:** Create a climax moment to wrap the 12 weeks.

- Drop a **full beat tape or EP with artist collabs**.
- Or a **limited-time exclusive beat pack** for sale (timed launch).
- Run a live session: **IG Live or YouTube Premiere** of the tape.
- Send final email: “My 12-Week Journey + What’s Next (and 5 New Beats).”

---

## **Ongoing Content System (Post Week 12)**

| Content Type | Frequency |
|--------------|-----------|
| Beat Previews (Reels/Shorts/TikTok) | 3–5x per week |
| Type Beat Upload (YouTube) | 1–2x per week |
| BTS / Studio Clips | Daily Stories |
| Email Newsletter | 2x per month |
| Collab Outreach / DM Strategy | 5–10 per week |
