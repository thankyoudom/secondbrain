A few thoughts on your current setup:
The hydrogen peroxide in multiple steps might be overkill and could irritate your gums over time. Once daily max is usually the recommendation, so maybe just keep it in your mouthwash or use it occasionally rather than in every step.
For whitening, I’d stick with your monthly strips rather than buying daily ones - you can use them 1-2x weekly for maintenance rather than switching to weaker daily strips. The daily strips are really designed for people who want gentler, everyday use, but since you’re already using the stronger monthly ones, just spacing them out works well.
Baking soda is great for whitening but abrasive, so limit it to a couple times weekly rather than daily.
Tongue scraper is definitely worth adding - helps with freshness and removes the coating that harbors bacteria.
