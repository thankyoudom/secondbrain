# Post Ideas

Love where your head’s at—developing your sound through content instead of chasing trends. You're building an aesthetic, not just numbers. Here’s a batch of simple, low-effort but high-vibe content ideas for producers like you, with minimal talking:

? Audio-Centric Content (No Talking)
Cassette Playback Clips
Record your beat playing off a cassette deck.
Zoom in on tape spinning, grainy texture, analog feel.
Add subtle VHS-style captions like “Loop 004 – Apr 22.”
“Studio Ambience” Vibes
Just the sounds of you turning knobs, loading plugins, or clicking pads.
Add subtle lo-fi beat in background or leave it raw.
Bonus if you film from a cool angle, low lighting, etc.
Playlists/Record Showcases
Film your hand scrolling through your private SoundCloud playlist, tapes, vinyl stack, or sample folders.
Overlay with beat snippets or ambient sounds.
Loop Sessions
15-second clips of loops on loop (literally).
Post as a “daily loop,” or just "No. 12 – Jazz Mood."
On-Screen Plugin Porn
Record plugin movements (delay throws, filter sweeps).
No commentary—just vibe.
Caption with what plugin it is + the feeling: “RC-20 on a rainy loop.”

? Visual + Aesthetic Content
Weekly Carousel Dump
Post 5–8 photos: studio, textures, food, city lights, cassette tapes.
Title: “Week 16 // sounds + sights”
Build visual identity that reflects your music.
"Sample Mood Board" Videos
Compile vintage clips, film grain overlays, text, sample audio.
Make it feel like a trailer for your next beat tape.
Slow Zoom Videos of Static Shots
E.g. your tape machine, a synth lit up in the dark, a beat playing off your phone in a park.
Add beat in background. Just one vibe per video.
“What I Sampled” Posts
Split-screen: OG sample vs your flip.
Don’t need to talk. Let the sound speak.
Your DAW Timeline Shots
Screen record 3 seconds of your FL timeline scrolling.
Drop subtle sound design or complex MIDI and leave people curious.

? Other Light-Lift Ideas
"No Context" Captions
Drop a post with just a strange caption or sample source like:
“sampled a 1972 Jamaican instructional tape”

Creates mystery, invites listens.
Before & After FX Posts
Play loop dry, then processed.
Side-by-side or stacked video.

? Bonus Idea – “Micro Projects”
### Start small themed series
“Lofi Loops” – 10 beat clips, same BPM/sample pool.
“April Tapes” – all cassette-dubbed clips for the month.
“Textures Only” – sample packs you made, turned into posts.
