# Perfect — here’s a full drum FX chain walkthrough in the same step…

Perfect — here’s a full drum FX chain walkthrough in the same step-by-step, beginner-friendly style. This will help you get the tight, bouncy, and gritty drums you hear in Kaytranada, Ivy Lab, and Erykah Badu's productions using free plugins.

Drum FX Chain (Free Plugin Version)
This works great for individual drum loops or a full drum bus (kick, snare, hats, etc.).

1. Drum Saturation / Punch (Softube Saturation Knob)
What it does: Adds character and punch by gently distorting your drums.
Why it matters: Makes snares crack and kicks thump harder, without sounding fake or “digital.”
### How to use
Drop Softube Saturation Knob on your drum bus.
Turn the big knob slightly to the right (start small).
### Set the switch to
Keep Low if you want fat kicks
Neutral if you're affecting full drums
Use your ears: it should make drums louder and fatter, not harsh.

2. Drum EQ (TDR Nova)
What it does: Balances the frequency content of your drums.
Why it matters: Removes mud, tames harsh snares, or adds clarity to hats.
### How to use
Load TDR Nova after Saturation.
### Basic settings
Low cut around 40–60 Hz if needed (careful with kick drum).
Boost at 100–120 Hz (for kick thump).
Cut around 400–500 Hz (if boxy).
Boost around 7–9 kHz (for snare crack / hi-hat shine).
Sweep frequencies slowly to find what sounds clean or nasty — go by ear.

3. Compression (TDR Kotelnikov)
What it does: Controls the dynamics — glues your drum sounds together.
Why it matters: Makes drums feel tighter and more controlled.
### How to use
Download TDR Kotelnikov.
Use on the drum bus or just the snare.
### Try
Threshold: Around -20 dB
Ratio: 2:1 to 4:1
Attack: 10–30 ms (lets transients hit)
Release: Auto or around 100 ms
Adjust makeup gain to bring the level back up.
Listen for a subtle punch — if it’s too squashed, back off the threshold.

4. Bus Reverb (Dragonfly Room or Valhalla Supermassive)
What it does: Adds a sense of space around snares or hats.
Why it matters: Ivy Lab and Erykah Badu often use roomy snares or echoey hats.
### How to use
Download Dragonfly Reverb (Room or Plate) or use Valhalla Supermassive.
Add it to an aux/send channel (so you can blend it).
Use a short decay (0.3–0.6s) for snappy room sound.
Send a bit of snare and hats into it (NOT the kick).
Helps your drums feel alive without drowning them.

5. Transient Shaping (Couture by Auburn Sounds)
What it does: Controls the attack and body of your drums.
Why it matters: Kaytranada-style kicks pop more, and snares snap harder.
### How to use
Install Couture Free.
Drop it on the drum bus, or on individual drums like snare or kick.
Turn Attack up slightly (1–3 dB) to sharpen the hit.
Use Sustain to control how long sounds last (try reducing it slightly).
Use this instead of or alongside compression for punch.

6. Lo-Fi / Texture (iZotope Vinyl or RC-20 Style)
What it does: Adds grit, dirt, or age to your drums.
Why it matters: Ivy Lab and Erykah Badu use crunchy, old-sounding textures.
### How to use
Use iZotope Vinyl or try Caelum Audio Tape Cassette 2.
### Try
Dust: 10–30%
Mechanical Noise: Low
Wear: Mid
Year setting: 1980 or 1970
Optional: use Spin Down for stop FX
Layer lightly, don’t drown the transients.

7. Bus Compression / Glue (Analog Obsession BUSTERse)
What it does: Final glue for the whole drum group.
Why it matters: Ties together all drum sounds for a pro feel.
### How to use
Download BUSTERse from Analog Obsession’s Patreon (free).
Place it at the end of your drum chain.
### Settings
Threshold: Adjust to hit ~3 dB of gain reduction
Ratio: 2:1
Attack: 10–30 ms
Release: Auto or 100 ms
Mix knob: Blend wet/dry signal

Bonus: Parallel Compression Bus (Optional for Boom)
Send your drums to a duplicate track.
Slam that one with heavy compression (10:1 ratio, -30 dB threshold).
Blend it back in at low volume.
Instant thickness without killing dynamics.

### Final Drum Chain Order
Softube Saturation Knob – warmth, color
TDR Nova – EQ shaping
TDR Kotelnikov – glue compression
Couture (Transient Shaper) – punch control
iZotope Vinyl – texture
Reverb Send (Dragonfly or Supermassive) – room feel
BUSTERse (Bus Glue) – final polish
