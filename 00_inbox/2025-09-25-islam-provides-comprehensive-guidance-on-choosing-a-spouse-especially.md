# Islam provides comprehensive guidance on choosing a spouse, especially…

Islam provides comprehensive guidance on choosing a spouse, especially emphasizing the qualities that contribute to a successful, harmonious, and righteous marriage.
For men choosing a wife, the primary and most important qualities to look for are religious commitment (Deen) and good character (Akhlaq).
The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said:
"A woman is married for four reasons: her wealth, her lineage, her beauty, and her religion. So marry the one with religion, may your hands be rubbed with dust (i.e., may you prosper)." (Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim)
Here is a breakdown of the key qualities Islam guides men to seek in a wife:

1. Religious Commitment (Deen) and Piety (Taqwa)

This is the foremost consideration. A religiously committed woman:
Fears God (Taqwa): Her consciousness of God will compel her to fulfill her duties and treat her husband and family with kindness and respect.
Encourages Good: She will support her husband in his religious life, encourage good deeds, and help create an Islamic environment for their children.
Upholds Responsibilities: She is one who will guard her chastity, honor, and her husband's property in his absence, as commanded in the Quran.

2. Good Character (Akhlaq) and Manners

A woman's character is essential for a peaceful home. This includes:
Kindness and Patience: She should be gentle, patient, and able to handle stress and disagreements wisely.
Obedience (in what is good): She is cooperative and respects her husband's leadership in the household, so long as it is not in contradiction to Islamic principles.
Affectionate and Loving (Wadu?d): The Prophet encouraged marrying women who are loving and affectionate to their husbands.

3. Fertility (Walu?d)

The Prophet also encouraged marrying women who are fertile and loving, as the growth of the Muslim nation is desired.

4. Other Secondary, but Permissible, Factors

While piety is the foundation, Islam recognizes and permits considering other factors:
Beauty/Physical Attraction: Attraction is an important component of a successful marriage as it is "more likely to create love between you." However, it should not be prioritized over religion and character.
Lineage/Family Background: A good, morally upright family background is beneficial as the person's upbringing significantly influences their values and character.
Common Sense and Compatibility: Having shared values, life goals, and the ability to reason and communicate effectively are important for long-term harmony.
In summary, the Islamic perspective guides a man to choose a wife who will be his partner in both this life and the Hereafter, building a home founded on faith, mutual respect, and love. The single most important advice is to prioritize the one who is religious and has good character.
