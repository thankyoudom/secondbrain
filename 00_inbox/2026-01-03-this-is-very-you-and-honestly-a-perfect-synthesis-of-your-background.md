# This is very you — and honestly a perfect synthesis of your background…

This is very you — and honestly a perfect synthesis of your background:
designer * producer * engineer * culture builder * future founder.
Let’s approach this the same way Virgil did:
culture first, product second, company third.

? Phase 1 — The Soul of the Brand (before names)
### From what I know about you
You are building a creative system, not just a clothing brand.
### Your world includes
Art objects & visual identity
Clothing / wearable artifacts
Album covers & music visuals
DJ sets / sound curation
Creative consulting & direction
Tech, health, data, infrastructure energy underneath it all
### So your brand should feel like
A studio + label + design house + culture lab
Not “merch”.

? Phase 2 — Your Signature Color System (like Virgil’s Canary Yellow)
Before names, the color philosophy matters because it becomes part of the language.
Virgil’s Canary Yellow = industrial caution + optimism + authority
### Let’s build yours from you
From your energy, your work, your interests, I see:
precision
cleanliness
future / tech
clarity
rhythm
human warmth under machine logic
Some directions your “Pantone moment” could live in:
Option A — Infra Blue
A deep, electric blue with warmth in it
Meaning: data + soul + night + motion
Option B — Pulse Orange
Soft but intense orange
Meaning: heart, motion, warmth, creativity, health
Option C — Signal Green
Neon-leaning green
Meaning: systems, life, infrastructure, growth, biotech
Option D — Void Violet
Dark purple with subtle glow
Meaning: mystery, sound, night, intelligence
We’ll lock one later — but this color becomes:
in your visuals
on your clothes
in your UI
in your album art
in your sets
in your identity language

? Phase 4 — First Principles of Your Brand
Before legal stuff, you need philosophy.
### Your brand should be built on
1?? Expression over product
You don’t sell clothes —
you release artifacts.
2?? Systems over seasons
Not “Spring/Summer drop”
but “Release 001: FIELD STUDY”
3?? World over logo
### Everything exists inside one universe
sound, fabric, image, motion, thought.
4?? Clarity over hype
Clean design, intentional releases, slow growth, deep meaning.

? Phase 5 — Making It “Official” (later step, but important)
When the soul is clear, the structure is simple:
Name locked
Domain secured
LLC formed
Basic trademark search
Simple website (manifesto + work + contact)
Brand book v1 (color, typography, language, philosophy)

? Now I need this from you
Answer these slowly — they will shape everything:
If someone experiences your brand, how should they feel?
(e.g. calm, inspired, seen, energized, curious, powerful, etc.)
Do you want the brand name to feel more:
A) Technical / futuristic
B) Poetic / human
C) Cultural / street
D) Spiritual / abstract
or a blend? Which blend?
How attached are you to the “7 / seven / 7dilla” lineage?
(Do you want “seven” energy inside the name, or was that a chapter?)
### Which direction excites you more
Art house / design studio
Culture label
Fashion + music hybrid
Creative technology studio
All of it under one umbrella?
Once you answer these,
we’ll lock the name, the color, and the identity.
