# Perfect—let’s dial this in for **music production + photography**, keep…

Perfect—let’s dial this in for **music production + photography**, keep it *light, playful, doable*, and still rooted in that *Artist’s Way* magic. You’re not looking to draw much, so we’ll lean into sound, texture, images, and exploration. I’ll reshape Week 1 for that creative combo, and we can expand week-by-week if this hits.

---

## **Week 1 – "Excavation + Permission to Play" (Music & Photo Edition)**

### **Daily Structure**
- **Morning Pages** *(daily)* – Clear the mental clutter. Think of it like tuning your creative instrument.
- **Artist Date** *(1x/week)* – Go solo somewhere visually or sonically inspiring (junk shop, park, music store, gallery).
- **Creative Exercise** *(daily)* – Play-based, low-pressure prompts using sound and/or image.

---

### **Daily Exercises (15–45 min)**

**Day 1 – Sound Walk & Photo Snap**
- **Task**: Take a walk, but listen *like* a field recordist. Snap 3–5 photos of textures, shadows, or scenes that “sound like something” to you.
- **Why**: Trains sensory crossover (aka *synesthesia vibes*) and builds sonic awareness.

**Day 2 – “Bad Beat” Session**
- **Task**: Open your DAW (or use voice memos/loops). Make the *weirdest*, “worst,” or goofiest beat possible.
- **Optional**: Add absurd lyrics or vocal effects.
- **Why**: Loosens perfectionism, reclaims play.

**Day 3 – Sonic Moodboard (Inspired by…)**
- **Task**: Choose a producer/artist you admire. Don’t listen to them today. Instead, make a 30-sec track or loop *inspired by* their vibe/aesthetic.
- **Why**: Build your *voice* by channeling—not copying—influence.

**Day 4 – Constraints Game: Loops & Limits**
- **Task**: Make a 1-minute beat/song using *only 3 samples* (or only drums/no melody, etc.).
- **Why**: Restrictions = creative goldmine.

**Day 5 – Visual-Sonic Collage**
- **Task**: Take photos around a theme (“decay,” “neon,” “soft,” etc.). Then create a short soundscape or beat that *feels like* those images.
- **Why**: Cross-medium inspiration boosts originality.

**Day 6 – Found Sound Flip**
- **Task**: Record a random sound (keys, fridge, footsteps). Chop it or use it as texture in a beat or ambient piece.
- **Why**: Makes the mundane magical.

**Day 7 – Reflection + Taste Audit**
- **Task**: Journal:
- What sounds felt fun to make?
- What surprised you?
- What do you want to explore more next week?
- **Bonus**: Write a letter *from* your inner artist/producer to future you.

---

### **Optional Tools to Make It Easy**
- **Voice Memos** – For quick ideas, field recording, beatbox layers.
- **DAW or app (Koala, GarageBand, etc.)** – Keep it simple.
- **Phone camera** – Use it like a visual sketchbook.
- **Pinterest / Moodboard App** – For taste tracking or Artist Date ideas.
