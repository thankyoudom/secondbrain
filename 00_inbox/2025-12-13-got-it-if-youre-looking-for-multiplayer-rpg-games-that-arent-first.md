# Got it! If you're looking for multiplayer RPG games that aren’t first…

Got it! If you're looking for multiplayer RPG games that aren’t first-person shooters but still offer a deep role-playing experience, here’s a list of top-rated, award-winning games that are trending right now:
1. Final Fantasy XIV
Release Date: 2010 (continually updated)
Platforms: PC, PS4, PS5
Why it's great: Final Fantasy XIV is one of the most popular MMORPGs in the world, offering a highly immersive world with rich lore, amazing visuals, and engaging combat. It features both PvE (player vs environment) and PvP (player vs player) content, with deep class and job systems, dungeons, and raids.
Awards: Numerous Game of the Year and Best MMO awards over the years. Its "Endwalker" expansion, in particular, received rave reviews for its storytelling and world-building.
Trending: Still highly active, with regular expansions and updates. The game has seen massive growth, especially after the "Realm Reborn" relaunch, and continues to expand its player base.
Bonus: Amazing social systems like crafting, gathering, and in-game player-driven economies make it a true MMO experience.
2. World of Warcraft
Release Date: 2004 (continually updated)
Platforms: PC
Why it's great: WoW remains the gold standard for MMORPGs. With hundreds of hours of content, including raids, dungeons, PvP, and more, WoW offers endless RPG progression and exploration. Its expansive world of Azeroth is filled with a wealth of content, from questing to crafting, and it has one of the most dedicated player communities in gaming.
Awards: WoW has won numerous accolades throughout its history, including Game of the Year and Best MMORPG in multiple years.
Trending: WoW is still very active with periodic expansions and updates. The "Dragonflight" expansion has breathed new life into the game, attracting both returning and new players.
3. Elder Scrolls Online
Release Date: 2014
Platforms: PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S
Why it's great: Set in the Elder Scrolls universe, ESO is an open-world MMORPG with a focus on exploration, quests, and character progression. Players can create their own unique characters and engage in PvE or PvP content. The game also has an excellent crafting system and allows players to join factions and participate in large-scale PvP battles.
Awards: ESO has received praise for its design and world-building, winning several MMO-related awards.
Trending: With continuous updates and new expansions (like the "High Isle" and "Necrom"), Elder Scrolls Online remains a key player in the MMORPG space.
4. Guild Wars 2
Release Date: 2012
Platforms: PC
Why it's great: Guild Wars 2 offers a dynamic world where player choices and actions impact the environment. The game features a unique event-driven system that makes the world feel alive, along with deep character customization and a mix of solo and group content. Its combat is fast and fluid, and it doesn’t require a subscription to play.
Awards: Known for its innovative design, Guild Wars 2 won several awards for its world-building, accessibility, and free-form questing.
Trending: While it has seen less frequent content updates than some of its competitors, Guild Wars 2 still has a loyal player base and continues to receive expansions like "End of Dragons."
5. Lost Ark
Release Date: 2022 (Western release)
Platforms: PC
Why it's great: Lost Ark is an action-packed MMORPG that combines RPG mechanics with fast-paced combat. It features a huge variety of classes, dungeons, and raids, and has an isometric camera view. The game also has elements of exploration and crafting, as well as a story-driven campaign. It’s especially well-known for its visually stunning environments and unique combat system.
Awards: The game has received praise for its design and graphics, particularly in Asia where it was first released.
Trending: Lost Ark became a huge hit in the West upon its release, with frequent updates and expansions keeping players engaged.
6. Diablo IV
Release Date: 2023
Platforms: PC, PS5, PS4, Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One
Why it's great: Diablo IV is an action RPG that focuses on dungeon crawling, looting, and intense combat. It features both solo and multiplayer content, with a vast open world, dynamic events, and deep customization. Players can team up in parties to explore dungeons, defeat powerful bosses, and collect loot to upgrade their characters.
Awards: Diablo IV received positive reviews for its engaging gameplay and world design, with its multiplayer features, dark narrative, and loot system earning praise.
Trending: Diablo IV has been a major hit since its release, with its seasonal updates and expansions ensuring it remains relevant in the action RPG genre.
7. Path of Exile
Release Date: 2013
Platforms: PC, PS4, Xbox One
Why it's great: Path of Exile is an online action RPG that focuses heavily on player choice, loot, and progression. Its skill tree is vast, allowing for nearly unlimited character customization. The game features a dark fantasy world, and while it doesn’t have traditional MMO elements, its economy and community-driven events create a multiplayer experience.
Awards: Frequently listed as one of the best action RPGs, Path of Exile has earned recognition for its depth and free-to-play model.
Trending: It’s still a strong contender in the ARPG space, especially with its seasonal leagues and constant updates.
8. Black Desert Online
Release Date: 2015
Platforms: PC, PS4, Xbox One, Mobile
Why it's great: Known for its breathtaking graphics and character customization, Black Desert Online features a sandbox world with intense PvP combat, as well as deep crafting, trading, and guild-based systems. The game’s combat system is action-oriented and fast-paced, making it stand out from other MMORPGs.
Awards: It has earned praise for its combat mechanics, graphics, and customization options.
Trending: Black Desert Online remains a popular choice for players who enjoy a high level of customization and a strong player-driven economy.
9. Monster Hunter: World
Release Date: 2018
Platforms: PC, PS4, Xbox One
Why it's great: Monster Hunter: World is a co-op action RPG where players hunt gigantic monsters in a massive open world. The game focuses on crafting, combat, and teamwork, and each hunt provides rewards that can be used to improve gear and skills. The online multiplayer experience lets you team up with others to take down powerful monsters together.
Awards: Monster Hunter: World received critical acclaim for its design, multiplayer experience, and action-oriented combat, winning numerous awards, including Game of the Year from several outlets.
Trending: Despite being a few years old, Monster Hunter: World continues to see updates and remains a fan favorite.
10. Sea of Thieves
Release Date: 2018
Platforms: PC, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S
Why it's great: Sea of Thieves is a pirate-themed action-adventure MMORPG with a focus on exploration and cooperation. Players form crews and sail the high seas, embarking on quests, looting treasures, and battling other players. The game offers a unique blend of multiplayer interactions and RPG elements like leveling up your pirate crew.
Awards: While its launch was rocky, Sea of Thieves has since evolved into a fan-favorite for its creative gameplay and fun interactions with other players.
Trending: Sea of Thieves has been continually updated, adding new events, quests, and expansions that keep the game feeling fresh and exciting.

### Honorable Mentions
No Man’s Sky – An expansive space exploration RPG that lets you team up with friends in multiplayer to explore the universe, craft, and engage in combat.
Star Wars: The Old Republic – A classic MMORPG set in the Star Wars universe, with both PvP and PvE content, and deep RPG progression.
Lost Ark – Another MMO with tons of content that blends action combat with deep character progression.
These games are all outstanding RPGs that aren’t first-person shooters but still provide engaging multiplayer experiences with deep storylines, progression systems, and cooperative (or competitive) gameplay. Let me know if you’d like to dive deeper into any of them!
