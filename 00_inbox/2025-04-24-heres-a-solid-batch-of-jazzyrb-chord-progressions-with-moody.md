# Here’s a solid batch of **jazzy/R&B chord progressions** with **moody…

Here’s a solid batch of **jazzy/R&B chord progressions** with **moody, mysterious, nonchalant** vibes—perfect for neo-soul, chill-hop, or sultry late-night grooves. I'll include the progressions in Roman numerals with some examples in a few keys too.

---

### **1. Mysterious & Jazzy**
**i7 - bVImaj7 - bVII13 - i7**
*(Example in A minor: Am7 - Fmaj7 - G13 - Am7)*
Floating and noir-like, great for setting an introspective mood.

---

### **2. Moody Soulful R&B**
**ii7 - V13 - Imaj9 - IVmaj7**
*(Example in C major: Dm7 - G13 - Cmaj9 - Fmaj7)*
Very smooth, classic R&B but still jazzy.

---

### **3. Nonchalant / Drifty**
**Imaj7 - iv7 - bVIImaj7 - V7sus4 - V7**
*(Example in E: Emaj7 - Am7 - Dmaj7 - B7sus4 - B7)*
Unexpected minor in the second chord gives it that lazy, cool feel.

---

### **4. Late-Night Lo-Fi Feel**
**ii7 - V7 - iii7 - vi9**
*(Example in Bb: Cm7 - F7 - Dm7 - G9)*
Subtle and lush. Works great with a swung or lazy groove.

---

### **5. Jazzy Suspense**
**i9 - IVmaj7 - bVII13 - bIIImaj7**
*(Example in D minor: Dm9 - Gmaj7 - C13 - Fmaj7)*
Mysterious and slick, good for storytelling or smoky vibes.

---

### **6. Dreamy & Emotional**
**Imaj9 - V7 - ii7 - IVmaj7**
*(Example in G: Gmaj9 - D7 - Am7 - Cmaj7)*
Wide open and a bit nostalgic.

---

### **7. Moody Modal Vibes**
**vi7 - ii7 - V7 - Imaj7**
*(Example in A: F#m7 - Bm7 - E7 - Amaj7)*
Subtle movement with a clean, jazzy resolution.

---

### **8. Smooth Turnaround**
**Imaj7 - bIII7 - ii7 - V7**
*(Example in C: Cmaj7 - Eb7 - Dm7 - G7)*
That unexpected bIII7 creates a jazzy surprise.

---

### **9. Eerie / Mellow Tension**
**i7 - bIImaj7 - iv9 - bVImaj7**
*(Example in A minor: Am7 - Bbmaj7 - Dm9 - Fmaj7)*
Dissonant but beautiful—good for something a little spooky but relaxed.

---

### **10. Jazzy Simplicity**
**IVmaj7 - Imaj7 - ii7 - V9**
*(Example in F: Bbmaj7 - Fmaj7 - Gm7 - C9)*
Laid-back and classic—versatile for any moody setting.
