? Pro Sound Design Trick (Free Only)
Render a melody to audio in FL
Reverse, slice, stretch or granulate it
Use PaulXStretch (free) or FL’s Granulizer to texture
Layer the reversed version under the original, low-passed, 30% volume
This creates dreamy Porter Robinson / ambient textures for free.
