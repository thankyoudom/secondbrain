## Overview of the Lexus SC300/400 Models

The Lexus SC300 and 400 were luxury grand tourers produced by Lexus, Toyota's luxury division. These models were introduced in the early 1990s as a response to the growing demand for high-performance sports cars with luxurious amenities. The SC300 was powered by a 3.0-liter inline-six engine, while the SC400 featured a 4.2-liter V8 engine.

## Key Features and Specifications

* Engine:
	+ SC300: 3.0-liter inline-six
	+ SC400: 4.2-liter V8
* Performance:
	+ SC300: 225 horsepower, 210 lb-ft torque
	+ SC400: 290 horsepower, 320 lb-ft torque
* Transmission:
	+ 6-speed manual (SC300)
	+ 4-speed automatic (SC400)
* Interior and Features:
	+ Luxurious interior with premium materials
	+ Advanced safety features for its time

## Significance and Legacy

The Lexus SC300 and 400 models played a significant role in establishing Lexus as a major player in the luxury automotive market. Their performance, comfort, and luxurious amenities set a new standard for high-end vehicles. Although production of these models ended in the early 2000s, they remain popular among collectors and enthusiasts due to their unique blend of style, performance, and refinement.
