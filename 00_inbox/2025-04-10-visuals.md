# visuals

Nice—if you're aiming for TikTok, eye-catching visuals that loop well and feel vibey (like vinyl, lo-fi CRT screens, moving waves, etc.) are key. Here are some **free visualizer tools/plugins/templates** you can use to create TikTok-ready visuals:

---

### **1. [Magic Music Visuals (Free Demo)](https://magicmusicvisuals.com/download)**
- **What it does**: Real-time music visualizer.
- **Vibe**: Customizable—waveforms, particle visuals, rotating vinyl.
- **Pros**: Works with audio input or files. Tons of tweakable FX.
- **Cons**: Watermark in the free version (might still be cool for TikTok if styled right).

---

### **2. [VSXu Player (Windows)](https://www.vsxu.com/)**
- **What it does**: Real-time audio reactive visuals.
- **Vibe**: Trippy, modular visuals—can feel VHS-y or spacey.
- **Use**: Run audio through it, screen record with OBS.

---

### **3. **Kapwing** + free visual assets**
- **What it does**: Online video editor (free tier).
- **Vibe**: Great for adding overlays like spinning vinyl, VHS filters, lofi loop gifs, etc.
- **Tip**: Grab free transparent vinyl PNGs or looping gifs (see below) and layer them.

---

### **4. [LottieFiles](https://lottiefiles.com/)**
- **What it does**: Animated graphics (JSON-based).
- **Use**: Search “vinyl”, “vibe”, “tape”, or “boombox”. Import into After Effects or Kapwing.
- Free and perfect for adding personality.

---

### **5. [OBS Studio + Plugins]**
- **What it does**: Live video capture with visual overlays.
- **Use**: Add visuals like:
- Spinning vinyl gifs
- Audio reactive bars (with a plugin like **Spectralizer**)
- Background videos (e.g., CRT/VHS overlays)

---

### **6. **DaVinci Resolve (Free)** with Lo-Fi Templates**
- **What it does**: High-quality editing.
- **Vibe**: You can add VHS overlays, tape static, and stylized text.
- **Where to get visuals**:
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/) (search “vinyl”, “lofi”)
- [Pexels Video](https://www.pexels.com/videos/)
- [Pixabay Video](https://pixabay.com/videos/)
