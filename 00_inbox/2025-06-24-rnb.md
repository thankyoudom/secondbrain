# Rnb

Here are real, effective platforms and strategies for promoting R&B remixes right now:

? Remix Submission Platforms & Curators
1. Digiwaxx Media (Hip-Hop & R&B)
A reputable campaign platform with frequent remix/beat contests.
You post and tag on TikTok/IG (e.g. @IamCarolDoche, @Digiwaxx), then submit via their site (digiwaxx.com, help.labelradar.com).
Ideal for gaining exposure through DJ networks, blogs, and social channels.

2. Artist Influence (SoundCloud Repost Network)
Over 50 million potential followers across genre-specific groups (e.g. Beats, R&B).
Free monthly submissions, then repost to curate their genre communities (artist.tools, artistinfluence.com).
Good for boosting plays on SoundCloud and building momentum.

3. Spotify Playlist Curators
Use tools like Artist.tools to find R&B-focused playlists:
Examples: “RnB ?” (27k followers, email contact available), “Rnb, Chill – Soulrise” (26k) (artist.tools).
Submit via email for playlist consideration.

4. PlaylistPartner (Remix + Electronic Focus)
Free submissions to remix/electronic playlists, often open to R&B remixes (playlistpartner.com).
Good way to land on curated playlists, especially on Spotify.

? Legal & Platform Best Practices
Use acapella stems and remix within copyright rules.
Avoid uploading to Spotify without permission — stick to SoundCloud, Mixcloud, TikTok with proper tagging (artist.tools, digiwaxx.com).
On IG or TikTok, activate Remix / Duet features and invite others to build upon your remix (blog.hootsuite.com).

? Community & Collaboration Strategies
Launch your own “Remix This” challenge: post a beat drop and caption “Stitch this with your verses.”
Tag small R&B vocalists or producers—build chain reactions.
Join Instagram/TikTok groups for remix collabs. As one Redditor said:
“Honestly, finding ‘remix this’ reels is kinda tough right now… Best bet is checking hashtags like #RemixThis or following accounts that post open verse stuff.” (reddit.com, artist.tools, forbes.com)

Use hashtags like
#rnbremix #rnbmusic #hiphopremix #producer #beatmaker #newmusic

— these are trending tags to reach niche audiences (blog.delivermytune.com).

? Suggested RnB Remix Growth Plan
Choose 1 R&B remix — prepare acapella stem + beat + short edit.
