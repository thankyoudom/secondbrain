DevOps Engineer
What you’ll do: Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines, automate deployments, and ensure that systems scale efficiently in the cloud. You’ll likely work with both GCP and tools like Terraform.
Why it's a fit: Terraform is central to DevOps work, and GCP will be your cloud environment.

What you’ll do: Design, implement, and manage cloud infrastructure on GCP. You’ll likely be tasked with using automation tools like Terraform to create and manage infrastructure as code.

Cloud Consultant (GCP)
What you’ll do: Help organizations migrate to GCP, optimize their existing GCP infrastructure, and implement automation with tools like Terraform.

Cloud Solutions Architect (Junior to Mid-Level)
What you’ll do: Design scalable and cost-efficient cloud solutions for clients, focusing on GCP. You’ll likely need to have experience in managing infrastructure and understanding the customer’s needs.
Why it's a fit: As you gain experience, this role would allow you to leverage both your certifications and technical know-how.
Skills to highlight: GCP services, solution design, cloud architecture, Terraform.
