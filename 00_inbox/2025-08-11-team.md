Often pairs well with ENTP or ENFP — they energize you, bring new possibilities, and keep life dynamic.

2 — Small Team Building for Your Next Chapter
You’re entering a platform + product phase, not just building solo. For a small but high-impact team, I’d look at people who amplify your core capabilities and cover blind spots. Think in terms of “accelerators,” not just “roles.”
Core Figures for the Next 12–18 Months
Ops/Execution Partner — Like a COO-lite for projects. Keeps timelines, manages client/admin load, so you can focus on high-value building.
Platform/Infra Engineer — A cloud/DevOps peer who’s deep in Terraform, Kubernetes, CI/CD. You can pair program, split architecture, and ship faster.
Content/Brand Strategist — Handles public-facing updates, helps you frame technical work in a way that attracts opportunities (Twitter, LinkedIn, case studies).
UX Researcher/Product Designer — Balances your engineering/design hybrid by running tests, validating ideas with real users, so you’re building the right thing.
Business Dev / Partnerships Lead — Spots collaborations, finds integrations, secures contracts or partnerships that multiply your reach.

Music Analogy (how it lines up with your past thought)
Sound Engineer ? Platform/Infra Engineer — they fine-tune the environment so the product “sounds” (performs) right.
Videographer ? Content Strategist — they capture and package your work so it’s seen and understood.
Stylist ? UX/Product Designer — makes sure what you release has the right feel and appeal.

If you built your team with just you + Ops Partner + Platform Engineer + Content Strategist, you’d have a tight 4-person unit capable of shipping products and showing them to the world effectively.
