Addressing the Art & Beats Validation Crisis
The moment you start seeing your creative work only as "streams of income," you introduce an external, uncontrollable variable (market success, audience acceptance). This clashes with your internal, INFJ need for purpose.
### A Stoic Reframe
Focus on Process (Internal): You control the time you spend drawing in Procreate and making beats. Dedicate time to it simply for the joy of creation (Taurus Venus comfort).
Release the Outcome (External): You cannot control if people buy the prints or license the beats. If income comes, view it as a bonus (preferred indifferent), not the primary goal.
Actionable Step: Schedule time for your art and beats before you schedule extra study time. Mark it as "Emotional Maintenance" or "Venus Practice," not "Side Hustle." This will legitimize its role in your foundation.
