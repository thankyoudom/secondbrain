Here’s a realistic example of a monthly income stream for an aspiring music producer using social media and light brand deals — mainly focusing on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram — with estimates based on known creator outcomes.

? GOAL: Earn $3,000/month as an aspiring music producer
Assumes you’re not yet famous but steadily building a niche audience around music production, beat making, or remixing. You’re consistently posting content and engaging with a community.

? Content Strategy & Output
TikTok: 4–5 short videos/week (18–20/month)
Beat flips, quick tutorials, studio vlogs, music challenges, duet/remix trends
15–60 second videos, optimized for trending sounds and hashtags
YouTube: 4–8 videos/month
2–4 Shorts (repurpose TikToks or make platform-specific ones)
2–4 Long-form videos: behind the beat, full production tutorials, vlogs, gear reviews
Instagram: 3–5 posts/week (12–20/month)
Reels (repurpose TikToks), beat snippets, aesthetic photos, swipe-through carousel posts
Stories: daily BTS, polls, Q&As, link to new drops or videos
