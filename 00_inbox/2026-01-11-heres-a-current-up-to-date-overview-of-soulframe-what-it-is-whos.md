# Here’s a current, up-to-date overview of Soulframe — what it is, who’s…

Here’s a current, up-to-date overview of Soulframe — what it is, who’s making it, what kind of gameplay and world it has, and what the community around it looks like as of early 2026:

? What Soulframe Is (and Isn’t)
Soulframe is an upcoming free-to-play MMORPG (massively multiplayer online RPG) in development by Digital Extremes, the studio best known for Warframe. (Gematsu)
It isn’t a standalone Souls-like (despite the name and some early speculation) — the “Soul” in Soulframe refers more to its narrative focus on souls and ancestral bonds, not Dark Souls-style combat. (PCGamesN)

? World & Gameplay
?? Open World Elements
The game features open world / open hub areas that you can explore on foot or with mounts. (Soulframe)
There are also procedurally generated underground caves and regions, which give variety to your exploration. (Soulframe)
Some zones act as persistent hubs where your progress (and where you logged out) remains unchanged. (Soulframe)
?? Combat & Systems
Combat leans more toward melee action than shooter mechanics, with a slower, deliberate pace. (Soulframe)
A Pact and Arcanics system shapes playstyles, giving passive and active abilities tied to your play choices. (Gematsu)
It blends action-RPG combat with MMO progression and exploration.

? Multiplayer & Social Gameplay
### Yes — it is multiplayer
The game is built as an online MMO where you can play alongside other players. (Gematsu)
Cooperative activity (co-op) is a core part of the design — you can team up for missions and PvE encounters. (GameSpot)
However, in current test builds the open world doesn’t yet automatically show other players unless you invite friends or group up — similar to how Warframe’s Relays work now. (Reddit)
Developers have stated they’re aiming for co-op that feels natural, without forcing players into multiplayer if they prefer to play solo. (GameSpot)
So yes, it’s multiplayer and supports social play, but matchmaking and shared open world presence are still works in progress as of its pre-release testing.

? Creators & Development
Developer & Publisher: Digital Extremes — same team behind Warframe. (Gematsu)
Creative Leadership: Directed by Steve Sinclair with Geoff Crookes and other leads involved. (Soulframe Wiki)
The game has been in ongoing Preludes / Pre-Alpha testing, with players getting in by signing up for codes on the official site. (Soulframe)
There’s no full release date yet, though testing has been active through 2025. (Soulframe)
Development has been iterative, with dev streams and community feedback helping shape features.

? Community & Culture
??? Current Community
There’s an active Discord and subreddit where players share experiences, bugs, builds, lore theories, and feedback. (Soulframe)
Discussions range from combat mechanics to how multiplayer should work and little exploration stories. (Reddit)
?? Community Engagement
Developers engage with players on Discord and in live dev streams (“Envoy Hour”), showing new content and responding to questions. (Soulframe)
Because the game is still in pre-release, player feedback significantly influences development direction — a point of pride for many in the community. (Reddit)
? “Cult Following”?
It’s not yet a massive mainstream hit (still in pre-release), but it does have a dedicated and passionate early player base — particularly from Warframe fans and lore enthusiasts who enjoy the worldbuilding and cooperative aspects. (Reddit)
The community is smaller than late-stage betas of big MMOs but very engaged and vocal about improvements and expectations.

?? Early Build Impressions
Players and outlets note that identity confusion about the game’s tone and genre (due to its name and comparisons) is a topic of conversation, but many enjoy the worldbuilding and art direction. (PCGamesN)
