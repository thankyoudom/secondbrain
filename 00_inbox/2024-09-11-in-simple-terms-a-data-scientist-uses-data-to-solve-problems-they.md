# In simple terms, a data scientist uses data to solve problems. They…

In simple terms, a data scientist uses data to solve problems. They collect, organize, and analyze large sets of data to find patterns, insights, and trends that help businesses or organizations make better decisions. They often use tools like statistics, programming, and machine learning to do this.

Personality-wise, someone well-suited for this role often has the following traits:
- **Curious**: Enjoys asking questions and finding answers through data.
- **Analytical**: Good at breaking down complex problems and thinking logically.
- **Detail-oriented**: Pays attention to small details to ensure accuracy.
- **Patient**: Data science can involve long, complex projects that require persistence.
- **Communicative**: Can explain complicated technical concepts to non-technical people.
- **Adaptable**: Comfortable learning new tools and methods as technology changes.

It’s a mix of technical and problem-solving skills with creativity.
