# Here is a more extensive list of countries that have not been colonized…

Here is a more extensive list of countries that have not been colonized in the traditional sense of being directly controlled and ruled by a foreign power:

1. **Japan**:
- Japan remained largely independent throughout its history, with the exception of a brief period of occupation by the United States after World War II. Despite being heavily influenced and pressured, Japan was never formally colonized.

2. **Thailand**:
- Thailand (formerly Siam) skillfully navigated the pressures of colonial powers in Southeast Asia and managed to avoid direct colonization. It maintained its sovereignty through diplomatic negotiations and reforms.

3. **Liberia**:
- Founded by the American Colonization Society as a homeland for freed African Americans, Liberia was never formally colonized by a European power, although it did experience significant influence and intervention from the U.S.

4. **Ethiopia**:
- Ethiopia is notable for its successful resistance against Italian colonization during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. It was briefly occupied by Italy from 1936 to 1941, but it was not fully colonized.

5. **Nepal**:
- Nepal maintained its independence during the British colonial era in India. It managed to avoid colonization through strategic diplomacy and military strength.

6. **San Marino**:
- San Marino, a small enclave within Italy, claims to be the world’s oldest republic. It maintained its independence throughout European history and was not colonized.

7. **Andorra**:
- Andorra, nestled between France and Spain, has remained independent and was never colonized, despite its proximity to larger powers.

8. **Monaco**:
- Monaco, a tiny city-state on the French Riviera, has preserved its sovereignty and avoided colonization, although it has been a French protectorate.

9. **Liechtenstein**:
- Liechtenstein, a small principality between Switzerland and Austria, has managed to avoid colonization and remains an independent entity.

10. **Vatican City**:
- The Vatican City has remained an independent and sovereign state throughout its history. It was not colonized and is governed by the Pope.

These countries have managed to retain their sovereignty through various means, including effective diplomacy, strategic geographic positions, and unique political or historical circumstances.
