# playlist

A good playlist length depends on the purpose of the playlist, but here are some general guidelines:
1. Casual Listening / Daily Use:
1–2 hours (15–30 songs)
Great for commutes, background music, or workouts. Keeps it fresh without feeling repetitive.
2. Event or Party Playlist:
3–5 hours (45–75 songs)
Long enough to avoid loops and accommodate changing moods or energy levels.
3. Focused Themes (e.g., Mood, Genre, Artist Highlights):
45 minutes–1.5 hours (10–25 songs)
Keeps the theme tight and engaging.
4. Road Trips / Long Listening Sessions:
5+ hours (75–100+ songs)
Great for extended periods when you don’t want to manually manage music.
Too short feels repetitive; too long can be overwhelming unless it’s meant to be shuffled. If you tell me the playlist’s purpose, I can give you a more tailored range.
