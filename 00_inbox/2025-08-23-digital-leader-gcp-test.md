# digital leader gcp test

If you're preparing for the Google Cloud Digital Leader (GCP Digital Leader) certification and looking for free practice tests, here are your top options:

1. Whizlabs – 20-Question Free Simulator
Offers a free test with 20 realistic questions, including multiple-choice and multiple-response types.
Mimics the actual exam environment with a live timer and performance report.
No negative marking; you can pause or exit and resume later.

2. Whizlabs Free Sample – 30 Practice Questions
A blog-style preview provides 30 sample questions spanning general cloud knowledge, GCP products, and infrastructure modernization.
Great for understanding the exam’s topics, though not in a timed simulator.
(Whizlabs)

3. TheExamsLab – Free Demo Questions
Offers a handful of demo questions for free in an online test format.
To access full sets, they charge around $55—but the demo lets you sample their format.
(theexamslab.com)

4. ExamCatalog – Updated Free Practice Test (Last updated July 7, 2025)
Publishes a few free practice questions with updated content relevant to the 2025 exam version.
Useful for checking current exam style and content.
(examcatalog.com)

5. Other Platforms (Zertly, ExamGecko, VMExam)
Zertly offers AI-powered unlimited practice after a free signup, tailored toward business users.
(zertly.com)
ExamGecko and VMExam provide sample or simulated questions and guides, but they may require registration or are limited in free access.
(examgecko.com, VMExam)
