# Congrats on getting your GCP Associate Cloud Engineer cert — that’s…

Congrats on getting your GCP Associate Cloud Engineer cert — that’s a solid milestone ?. Let’s break down your situation realistically:

? What the ACE cert gives you
Credibility: It signals you understand GCP’s fundamentals (VMs, networking, IAM, storage, deployments, monitoring). Recruiters do keyword-scan for this cert.
Edge over non-certified applicants: At the junior level, certs can make the difference in getting a first interview.
But not a guarantee: Companies will still want to see applied skills — that’s where your 3 projects come in.

? Projects to Prioritize
If you want the cert to “translate” into jobs, make your 3 projects resume- and GitHub-ready. Good examples for juniors:
Deploy a web app on GCP: Containerize a simple app (Node.js, Python, etc.) ? deploy on Cloud Run / GKE with CI/CD.
Data pipeline: Use Pub/Sub + Dataflow/BigQuery to process & visualize sample data.
Infra as Code: Set up a VPC, IAM roles, storage, and a VM using Terraform.
These check multiple hiring boxes: cloud fundamentals, automation, and applied problem-solving.

?? Work experience factor
Even a bit of tech work experience (IT, support, scripting, etc.) is an asset.
Frame it in cloud-adjacent terms (e.g., “automated deployments,” “managed VM resources,” “configured IAM-like policies”).

? Applying in South Florida
South Florida’s cloud job market is thinner than Chicago’s. You’ll mostly see IT, fintech, healthcare, and some startup roles.
You’ll likely find more DevOps/SRE hybrid positions than “pure” junior cloud engineer titles.

? Chicago Reality
Chicago does have more cloud roles: finance, healthcare, consultancies, and a healthy tech scene.
Relocation for junior roles is less common, but not impossible:
Some companies will take a chance if you’re already planning to move and can say “I’ll be local.”
A stronger angle is remote-first junior roles — but competition is fierce.
If you want Chicago, you could either:
Start applying now, mention in cover letters “I am relocating to Chicago and available on-site,” or
Land something remote/hybrid from Florida and then transfer later.

? Action Plan (next 2–3 months)
Polish LinkedIn + GitHub with cert + projects.
Apply now — don’t wait. Applications ? interviews ? offers can take months.
Target junior cloud, DevOps, or platform engineer roles.
Join GCP + local tech meetups (Miami has some, Chicago has plenty — even virtual ones matter).
Consider contract roles or consultancies — easier entry points than FANG-type companies.
