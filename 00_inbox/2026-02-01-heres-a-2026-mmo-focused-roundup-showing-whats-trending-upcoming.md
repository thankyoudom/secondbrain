# Here’s a 2026 MMO-focused roundup showing what’s trending, upcoming…

Here’s a 2026 MMO-focused roundup showing what’s trending, upcoming, and award-winning across the genre — from established giants to hot new worlds worth watching: (Indie Kings)

? 2026 Trending / Most Played MMOs & Expansions
? World of Warcraft: Midnight
Massively multiplayer expansion releasing March 2, 2026 with new zones, player housing, narrative pushes and experimental modes. (Wikipedia)
Blizzard calls Midnight one of their most significant expansions in years. (GamesRadar+)
? RuneScape & Old School RuneScape
Traditional MMO still evolving — OSRS has a 2026 roadmap with major new skills, PvP events and the first new raid in four years. (GamesRadar+)
RuneScape also grabbed Best Indie MMO and Most Improved MMO (shared) in 2025 community awards. (MMORPG.com)
