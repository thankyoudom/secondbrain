# League of Legends has actually been free-to-play since it launched…

League of Legends has actually been free-to-play since it launched back in 2009! That’s been their business model from the start.
The game itself costs nothing to download and play - you get access to all the core gameplay. They make money through:
|  | ? | Skins (cosmetic changes to how champions look) |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | ? | Champion unlocks - you start with a rotating selection of free champions each week, and can unlock others permanently by playing and earning in-game currency, or by buying them with real money to speed things up |
So yeah, you can play League completely free forever. You’ll just need to either grind a bit to unlock champions you want, or spend money if you want them all immediately. But the game itself and all its competitive features are totally free.
It’s one of the biggest free-to-play success stories and probably has one of the largest gaming communities in the world. The subreddit and Discord communities are massive if you’re looking to connect with other players.
