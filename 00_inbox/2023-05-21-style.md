The Ruler
Sophistication, authority and an air of power.
Rich, dark colours: Deep hues such as navy blue, black, burgundy, and forest green convey a sense of authority and command.
Luxurious fabrics: High-quality materials like silk, wool, and cashmere emphasise the importance of the wearer.
Tailored fits: Well-fitted and structured garments, like blazers, suit jackets, and pencil skirts, create a polished and professional appearance.
Classic patterns: Subtle patterns like pinstripes, herringbone, or plaid add visual interest while maintaining the style’s sophisticated appeal.
Statement accessories: Bold jewelry, such as a statement necklace, cufflinks, or a luxury watch, can be used to accentuate the Ruler archetype.
Formal footwear: Elegant shoes, like leather oxfords, brogues, or high-heeled pumps, complement the overall look.
Power pieces: Items that denote status, such as a tailored suit, a designer handbag, or a well-made coat, reinforce the Ruler archetype’s commanding presence.

Green (Secondary Color) #
Green is a very down–to–earth color. It can represent new beginnings and growth. It also signifies renewal and abundance. Alternatively, green can also represent envy or jealousy, and a lack of experience.

Green Color Psychology

In color psychology, green is highly connected to nature and money. Growth, fertility, health, and generosity are some of the positive color meanings for the color. The color meaning for green also carries some negative associations such as envy.

C-suite
This classification of roles covers high-ranking executives within an organization. The ‘C’ in c-suite stands for chief. In general, there's a trend for the c-suite to build data-driven decision making into their processes. Individuals filling these roles within organizational leadership teams are expected to be familiar with data and analytics.
Key attributes
What they do: Responsible for data and data professionals across an entire organization
How they do it: They are decision makers found at the top end of a company’s hierarchy
Examples of job titles

Chief Technology officer
Chief marketing officer
Chief data officer
Chief analytics officer
Chief information officer
Chief data scientist

Islam encourages individuals to engage in activities that are beneficial, constructive, and within the bounds of Islamic principles. There are numerous hobbies and activities that are considered halal (permissible) for men. Here are some examples:
Sports and physical activities: Engaging in sports, such as swimming, running, cycling, soccer, basketball, martial arts, or any other physical activities that promote good health, fitness, and discipline are considered permissible.
Reading and learning: Pursuing knowledge and intellectual growth through reading books, articles, or studying various subjects that are beneficial to oneself and the community is encouraged in Islam.
Writing: Writing poetry, fiction, non-fiction, or expressing oneself through creative writing is a permissible hobby.
Gardening: Cultivating plants, flowers, or vegetables can be a rewarding and enjoyable hobby while connecting with nature and appreciating Allah's creation.
Cooking and culinary arts: Preparing food, exploring different cuisines, and developing culinary skills is a permissible hobby.
Photography and painting: Engaging in photography or painting while avoiding the creation of images depicting living beings (in the case of painting) is considered permissible.
Calligraphy: Learning and practicing the art of Arabic calligraphy is a revered and permissible hobby in Islamic culture.
Volunteering and community service: Engaging in charitable activities, volunteering at local organizations, or participating in community service projects that help those in need are highly encouraged.
Learning and playing musical instruments: While there are differing opinions among scholars, learning and playing musical instruments that are not explicitly prohibited by Islamic teachings is considered permissible by many scholars.
Outdoor activities: Going for hikes, camping, fishing, or exploring nature while adhering to Islamic principles and maintaining modesty is permissible.
It's important to note that the permissibility of a particular hobby can vary depending on cultural norms, personal circumstances, and individual interpretations. Muslims are encouraged to seek knowledge, consult with knowledgeable scholars, and engage in activities that promote personal growth, positive values, and benefit both themselves and society as a whole.

The Magician
An air of mystique and sophisticated minimalism.
Rich, dark or light colours: Deep hues like navy, burgundy, and emerald green, as well as metallic shades such as gold and silver, evoke a sense of power and enchantment.
Luxurious fabrics: Materials like silk, velvet, and brocade contribute to the sumptuous and elegant feel of the Magician archetype.
Dramatic cuts: Asymmetrical designs, flowing capes or sleeves, and high collars create an air of mystique and theatricality.
Futuristic patterns and tech-inspired elements: Geometric shapes, holographic or metallic materials, and innovative textiles can add a modern, cutting-edge appeal to the clothing.
Layering: Layered garments and accessories, like scarves, shawls, or vests, provide depth and complexity to the overall look.
Bold accessories: Statement jewellery, such as large rings, pendant necklaces, or elaborate headpieces, can emphasise the Magician’s mystical allure.
Distinctive footwear: Heeled boots, ornate sandals, or other eye-catching shoes complement the overall style.

3. Reduce the sum to a single digit: 3 + 9 = 12, and then 1 + 2 = 3.
