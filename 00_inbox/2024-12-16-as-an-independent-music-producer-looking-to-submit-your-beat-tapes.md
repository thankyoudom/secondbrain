# As an independent music producer looking to submit your beat tapes…

As an independent music producer looking to submit your beat tapes, here are several platforms on YouTube and Instagram that accept submissions:

1. **Chill Moon Music**: An Instagram-based platform that periodically accepts beat tape submissions. For example, they had a submission window ending on October 15, 2023. Keep an eye on their Instagram page for future opportunities.

2. **Taetro**: A YouTube channel that invites music and beat submissions for review. You can submit your work by following the instructions provided in their video descriptions.

3. **BeatStars**: While primarily a platform for selling beats, BeatStars also curates playlists and accepts track submissions. You can learn more about the submission process through their YouTube tutorials.

4. **Promoting Sounds**: A YouTube channel with a significant following that accepts music submissions. Due to high volume, not all submissions are featured, but it's worth submitting your work for consideration.

5. **Accurate Beats**: An Instagram account where the producer shares beat tapes and projects. While not explicitly stated, reaching out for collaboration or submission opportunities could be beneficial.

6. **Busy Works Beats**: A YouTube channel offering music production tutorials and occasionally featuring user-submitted content. Engaging with their community might open up submission opportunities.

7. **DistroKid**: While not a promotional platform, DistroKid offers distribution services to release your beat tapes on various streaming platforms, which can complement your promotional efforts.

8. **CJ Beats**: A YouTube channel that accepts beat and song submissions for review. You can send your work to the email provided in their video descriptions.

9. **Music Promotion Channels on Reddit**: Communities like r/makinghiphop discuss various YouTube channels that accept music submissions. Engaging with these communities can provide leads on where to submit your beat tapes.

10. **Instagram Music Curators**: Many Instagram accounts curate and share music from independent artists. Researching and reaching out to these curators can provide additional avenues for submitting your beat tapes.

Remember to review each platform's submission guidelines and deadlines to increase the chances of your beat tapes being accepted and featured.
